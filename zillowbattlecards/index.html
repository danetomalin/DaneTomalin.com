Realtor.com

realtor.com
"The official portal backed by NAR and Move Inc/News Corp"
Realtor.com, operated by Move Inc (News Corp subsidiary), is the official portal endorsed by the National Association of Realtors. In 2026, Realtor.com continues aggressive positioning around ReadyConnect and lead-generation sales to agents, with a focus on higher-quality vetting through call-center concierge services. However, Realtor.com's commission-based lead model and heavy reliance on NAR trust creates competitive friction against Zillow's Premier Agent subscription approach. Realtor.com's market position is strong but increasingly challenged by Zillow's direct-to-agent positioning and Zillow's Premier Agent subscription offering compelling lead delivery at predictable cost.

Elevator Pitch (Zillow Premier Agent)

For agents tired of paying per-lead with unpredictable ROI on Realtor.com's commission model, Zillow Premier Agent delivers a flat-rate subscription with full control over your leads—no vetting gatekeepers, no NAR middleman, no surprise cost increases.

Value Proposition vs. Realtor.com

Functional: flat-rate monthly subscription (vs. Realtor.com's per-lead commission model) with transparent pricing and direct lead ownership. Monetary: predictable customer acquisition cost with no variable commission, plus Zillow Showcase enables agents to showcase listings and capture additional leads on Zillow's massive 100M+ monthly traffic. Psychological: Zillow positions agents as business owners, not users of an NAR-blessed portal—control your lead flow, manage your follow-up, own your data.
