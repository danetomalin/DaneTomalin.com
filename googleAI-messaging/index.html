"For CDOs and enterprise AI leaders who need production-scale outcomes from vertical AI in healthcare and consumer experiences, Google Cloud is the Industry & Applied AI platform that turns frontier models into compliance-ready, revenue-attached solutions deployable in weeks."
Vertical Applied AI

Messaging Principles

📊
Lead with Outcomes
Start with business impact (time-to-production, compliance assurance, ROI proof) before mentioning features.
🎯
Use Specific Numbers
Avoid generics like 'faster' or 'easier.' Use exact metrics: 6-week deployment, $2M+/year savings, 340% OCR surge.
🛡️
Name Competitive Gaps
Position against Microsoft bundling, Salesforce lock-in, Anthropic safety-first narrowness—show the full-stack advantage.
🧠
Match Buyer Brain
CDOs want compliance-by-default. CFOs want ROI proof. CIOs want model flexibility. Language shifts by seniority.
🚫
Compliance is Outcome
Regulatory readiness isn't technical overhead—it's a competitive advantage. Lead with audit trails, governance pre-built.
✨
Show Production Scale
Customer wins (CVS, Humana, Gap), not pilots. Published outcomes, multi-site deployments, board-reportable metrics.

Audience Profiles

Chief Digital Officer / Head of Enterprise AI
Speaks Natively
OCR compliance, time-to-production, multi-model governance, board-reportable ROI
Background
Digital transformation, regulated industry (healthcare, financial services), enterprise infrastructure
Wants
Vertical solutions that deploy in weeks, compliance-by-default, multi-model flexibility, executive visibility
Language to Use
• "From zero to production in 6 weeks with compliance pre-built"
• "Model flexibility without vendor lock-in"
• "BigQuery + Vertex AI unified governance"
Pressure
Defending Industry AI spend to CFO, proving it's not just reach
Wants
Defensible Industry AI strategy, cross-device measurement, competitive positioning
Language to Use
• "Stop defending with reach curves"
• "Show exact conversion attribution"
• "Board-ready ROI proof"
CFO / Board Member
Mindset
Every dollar defensible, quantifiable impact, no hand-waving
Red Flags
Reach curves, unmeasured spend, vendor lock-in, undefined ROI
Wants
Exact conversions, ROAS range, fraud prevention, scalability proof
Language to Use
• "$4.50-$8.50 ROAS"
• "Eliminate $7.4B fraud problem"
• "20-30% YoY ROAS compounding"
Media Buyer / VP Media (Legacy)
Native Language
Reach, frequency, GRPs, insertion orders, negotiations
Challenge
Trained on linear, still thinking in impressions not outcomes
Wants
Bridge language, performance framework, programmatic efficiency
Language to Use
• "45% migration from linear"
• "Performance > reach"
• "No weekly IO negotiations"
Performance Agency Leader
Motivation
New revenue stream, client differentiation, higher margins
Challenge
TTD commoditized, need white-label differentiation
Wants
White-label platform, outcome-based pitch, recurring revenue model
Language to Use
• "$2M-$10M Industry AI revenue stream"
• "Your proprietary Industry AI engine"
• "Margin expansion on media spend"
